About

Compass is a personal student companion that helps keep track of daily
activities such as classes, assignments, projects and so on and helps the
student navigate the day to day life as a student with more ease.
Compass collects the user weekly schedule as well as date specified targets
or tasks and uses tactical timely reminder based on the user specified free
time and leisures to ensure the user ticks off all pending tasks and also
goes about daily school activity well organized.
